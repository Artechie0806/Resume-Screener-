How It Works

This Resume Screener uses an AI model to classify resumes and match them with your selected job role. Upload one or more resumes, select your target role, and click Analyze. The system will identify the top relevant roles and tell you whether the candidate is a fit.

  • - Select a target role (e.g., Data Scientist, HR)
  • - Upload resumes (PDF or TXT format)
  • - The AI shows the top 3 predicted roles for each resume
  • - If any prediction matches your target role, it's marked as Fit
